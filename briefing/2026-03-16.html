Chess
The Zwischenzug
An "in-between move" — instead of recapturing immediately, you play a surprising intermediate move (often a check or threat) that changes the position before recapturing. Most players miss it. When your opponent seems forced to recapture, look for a zwischenzug first.
Piano
Voicing chords
In a chord, bring out the melody note (usually the top) by pressing it slightly firmer than the inner voices. The inner notes should support, not compete. Practice playing a chord and listening for the melody to "float" above the harmony.
